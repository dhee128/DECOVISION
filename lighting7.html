Lighting Ideas

String Lights

Choose a modern, minimalist design for a contemporary feel or a more ornate, traditional style for a classic look. A chandelier with adjustable dimming options allows you to set the perfect mood.

Lanterns

Floor lamps are perfect for adding both style and extra lighting to your living room. Opt for a tall, sleek design for a modern touch, or choose a vintage-style tripod lamp for a more eclectic vibe.

LED Strip Lights

Table lamps can add warmth and intimacy to the living room. Place them on side tables next to the sofa or near a reading nook.

Solar-Powered Lights

Recessed lighting, also known as can lights, can be installed in the ceiling to provide a clean, unobtrusive light source.

Hanging Pendant Lights

Wall sconces are an elegant option for adding soft, ambient lighting to the walls. They can be placed on either side of a sofa or near a doorway to create a welcoming atmosphere.

Candlelight

LED strip lights can be installed along the edges of the ceiling or behind furniture to create a subtle, ambient glow.
