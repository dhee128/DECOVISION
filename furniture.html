Furniture Ideas

Modern Sectional Sofa

A sleek, L-shaped sofa with neutral upholstery and clean lines. Includes hidden storage compartments or an optional chaise for lounging.

Mid-Century Coffee Table

A wooden coffee table with tapered legs and a smooth finish. Features open shelving or drawers for additional storage.

Accent Armchair

A cozy armchair with a bold fabric pattern or vibrant color. Adds a focal point to the room while offering extra seating.

Multi-Functional TV Stand

A stylish stand with a mix of open shelves and closed cabinets. Perfect for organizing electronics, books, and decor items.

Ottoman with Storage

A plush ottoman that doubles as a storage unit for blankets or magazines. Can also serve as a footrest or additional seating.

Bookshelves/Storage Units

Bookshelves or storage units help keep your living room organized and functional. They can also double as decorative pieces by displaying books, plants, or artwork. Open shelving is great for a modern look, while closed storage units can help hide clutter.
