Plants Ideas

Fiddle Leaf Fig

The Fiddle Leaf Fig is a popular choice for living rooms due to its large, glossy leaves and dramatic appearance.

Snake Plant

Snake plants are low-maintenance and can thrive in low light, making them ideal for areas with limited natural sunlight.

Peace Lily

Peace Lilies are known for their elegant white blooms and air-purifying properties. They do well in indirect light and can be placed on a side table, shelf, or in a decorative pot.

Pothos

Pothos is a versatile and easy-to-care-for plant that can be hung in decorative planters or allowed to trail down from shelves.

Spider Plant

Spider plants are perfect for adding a bit of texture and greenery to your living room. Their long, arching leaves and small "babies" that hang from the plant make them visually interesting.

Rubber Plant

The Rubber Plant is another large-leafed plant that adds a touch of elegance to the living room. With its deep green, glossy leaves, it can be placed in a decorative pot to enhance the room's aesthetic.
